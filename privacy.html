Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

Overview

Waqty ("we", "our", "us") is a prayer times app designed to help you stay on time with your daily prayers. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you share with us.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to operate the service:

  • Email address — collected only when you voluntarily join our waitlist. Used solely to notify you when the app launches.
  • Prayer time location data — when you use the app, your location is used to calculate accurate prayer times. This data is processed on your device and is not stored on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

  • To send you a one-time launch notification if you joined the waitlist.
  • To calculate prayer times based on your location (on-device only).

Data Storage

Waitlist email addresses are stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. We do not sell, rent, or share your email address with any third parties.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website does not use cookies, analytics, or any tracking technologies.

Third-Party Services

We do not share your personal data with any third-party services for marketing or advertising purposes.

Data Retention

Waitlist emails are retained until the app launches and the notification is sent. You may request deletion of your email at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@getwaqty.app.
